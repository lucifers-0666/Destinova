Frequently Asked Questions

You can cancel your booking through your account dashboard or by contacting our 24/7 customer support team. Cancellation charges may apply depending on the airline's policy and timing of cancellation.

Refund policies vary by airline and fare type. Most refundable tickets are processed within 7-10 business days. Non-refundable tickets may incur cancellation fees. We recommend purchasing travel insurance for added protection.

Baggage allowance varies by airline, route, and fare class. Typically, economy class includes 1 checked bag (23kg) and 1 carry-on (7kg). Business and first class passengers enjoy higher limits. Check your booking confirmation for specific details.

Online check-in typically opens 24-48 hours before departure. Visit the airline's website or use their mobile app to check-in. You'll need your booking reference and passenger details. We also send check-in reminders via email.

Yes, seat selection is available through your booking management page or directly on the airline's website. Some airlines offer free seat selection, while others charge a fee for preferred seats. Premium cabin passengers typically enjoy complimentary seat selection.
