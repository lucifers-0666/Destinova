FAQ

Flight Expiration — Quick Answers

Does “fare expires in 24h” mean my booking is safe?

That clock reflects the price hold; your PNR can purge earlier if TTL is shorter for your fare.

What if I pay after TTL?

The reservation may be canceled; you must re‑price and re‑book if seats remain.

Is ticket validity always one year?

No — many standard fares allow up to 12 months, but promotional brands can be stricter.

When is no‑show triggered?

Missing check‑in/bag‑drop or boarding cutoff can forfeit coupons per fare rules.
