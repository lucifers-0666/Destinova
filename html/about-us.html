FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Destinova

What makes Destinova different from other booking platforms?

Destinova combines cutting-edge AI technology with personalized service to deliver a truly luxurious booking experience. We offer complete price transparency, 24/7 support, and exclusive partnerships with 500+ airlines to ensure you always get the best value.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption and are fully PCI DSS compliant. Your payment data is never stored on our servers and all transactions are processed through secure, certified payment gateways.

What if I need to cancel or modify my booking?

We offer flexible cancellation policies depending on your fare type. Our 24/7 support team is always available to assist with modifications, cancellations, or any special requests. Most changes can be made instantly through our mobile app or website.

Do you charge any hidden fees?

Never. Transparency is one of our core values. The price you see during booking is the final price you pay—no surprises, no hidden charges. All taxes, fees, and surcharges are clearly displayed upfront.

How can I contact customer support?

Our elite support team is available 24/7 through multiple channels: phone (+91 123 456 7891), email (support@destinova.com), live chat on our website and app, or through our social media channels. We're always here to help.
