FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking through the "Manage Booking" section on our website. You will need your booking reference number and the last name of the primary passenger. Please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on the fare rules.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are processed based on the fare conditions of your ticket. Refundable tickets will be credited back to the original payment method within 7-10 business days. Non-refundable tickets may be eligible for a travel credit.

What is the baggage allowance?

Baggage allowance varies by travel class and destination. Typically, Economy class allows one checked bag up to 23kg, while Business and First Class offer more generous allowances. You can find specific details on your e-ticket.

How can I check-in online?

Online check-in opens 48 hours before your flight's departure. Visit the "Check-in" page on our website and enter your booking reference and last name to proceed.

Can I select my seat after booking?

Yes, you can select or change your seat through the "Manage Booking" portal. Some seats may require an additional fee depending on your fare type.
