## 1. Getting Started

### What is Destinova?

Destinova is an online flight booking platform that allows you to:
- Search and compare flights from multiple airlines
- Book domestic and international flights
- Select your preferred seats
- Make secure online payments
- Manage your bookings anytime, anywhere

### System Requirements

To use Destinova, you need:
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A stable internet connection
- JavaScript enabled in your browser

---

## 2. Creating an Account

Creating an account gives you access to exclusive features like saved travelers, booking history, and price alerts.

### Steps to Register:

1. **Click "Sign Up"** in the top navigation bar
2. **Fill in your details:**
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address (must be valid - you'll need to verify it)
   - Password (must contain uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character)
   - Phone Number (optional but recommended)

3. **Click "Create Account"**

4. **Verify your email:**
   - Check your inbox for a verification email
   - Click the verification link
   - Your account is now active!

### Password Requirements:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

## 4. Searching for Flights

### One-Way Flight Search:

1. **Go to the Homepage**
2. **Select "One Way"** trip type
3. **Enter flight details:**
   - **From:** Type your departure city or airport code (e.g., Delhi or DEL)
   - **To:** Type your destination city or airport code
   - **Departure Date:** Select from the calendar
   - **Passengers:** Choose number of travelers
   - **Class:** Economy, Business, or First Class

4. **Click "Search Flights"**

### Round-Trip Search:

1. Select **"Round Trip"**
2. Enter departure and destination
3. Select **Departure Date** and **Return Date**
4. Choose passengers and class
5. Click **"Search Flights"**

### Multi-City Search:

1. Select **"Multi-City"**
2. Add multiple flight legs
3. For each leg, enter:
   - Origin
   - Destination
   - Date
4. Click **"Add Another Flight"** to add more legs
5. Click **"Search Flights"**

### Search Filters:

After searching, refine results using filters:
- **Price Range:** Set min/max budget
- **Airlines:** Select preferred airlines
- **Departure Time:** Morning, Afternoon, Evening, Night
- **Stops:** Non-stop, 1 stop, 2+ stops
- **Duration:** Maximum flight duration

### Sorting Options:
- **Price:** Lowest to highest
- **Duration:** Shortest first
- **Departure:** Earliest first
- **Arrival:** Earliest first

## 6. Selecting Seats

### How Seat Selection Works:

1. After selecting a flight, click **"Select Seats"**
2. View the **interactive seat map**
3. **Seat Colors:**
   - üü¢ **Green:** Available
   - üîµ **Blue:** Selected by you
   - ‚ö´ **Grey:** Occupied/Unavailable
   - üü° **Yellow:** Premium seats (extra cost)

### Selecting Seats:

1. Click on an available seat
2. The seat will highlight and price will display
3. Assign seat to a passenger
4. Repeat for all passengers
5. Click **"Confirm Seats"**

### Seat Types:

- **Window Seat:** Great for views, marked with "W"
- **Middle Seat:** Between window and aisle
- **Aisle Seat:** Easy access, marked with "A"
- **Exit Row:** Extra legroom, restrictions apply
- **Front Rows:** Less walking, extra charge

### Seat Selection Tips:
- Book early for better seat selection
- Exit row seats require ability to assist in emergencies
- Families traveling together should select adjacent seats

## 7. Making Payment

### Accepted Payment Methods:

- **Credit/Debit Cards:** Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **UPI:** Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm
- **Net Banking:** All major banks
- **Wallets:** Paytm, Amazon Pay

### Payment Process:

1. **Select Payment Method**
2. **Enter payment details:**
   - For cards: Card number, expiry date, CVV
   - For UPI: Enter UPI ID or scan QR code
   - For Net Banking: Select your bank

3. **Review payment amount**
4. **Click "Pay Now"**
5. **Complete authentication:**
   - OTP for cards
   - UPI PIN
   - Bank credentials

6. **Wait for confirmation**

### Payment Security:

- All payments are processed through **Stripe** (PCI-DSS compliant)
- Your card details are **never stored** on our servers
- Look for the üîí lock icon in your browser
- **3D Secure** authentication for added protection

### After Successful Payment:

1. You'll see a **confirmation page**
2. **Booking reference number** will be displayed
3. **E-ticket** will be sent to your email
4. You can also download it from **My Bookings**

- You'll receive a **cancellation confirmation** email
- **Refund** will be processed within 5-7 business days

## 11. Setting Price Alerts

### What are Price Alerts?

Get notified when flight prices drop for routes you're interested in!

### Creating a Price Alert:

1. Go to **"Price Alerts"** in your dashboard
2. Click **"Create New Alert"**
3. Enter alert details:
   - **From:** Departure city
   - **To:** Destination city
   - **Target Price:** Your budget
   - **Travel Date:** Specific or flexible

4. Click **"Set Alert"**

### Alert Notifications:

- Receive **email notifications** when prices drop
- Alerts check prices **daily**
- Maximum **10 active alerts** per account

### Managing Alerts:

- **Edit:** Change target price or dates
- **Pause:** Temporarily disable without deleting
- **Delete:** Remove alert permanently

## 14. Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset via email.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**  
A: Contact customer support to change your registered email.

**Q: Why is my account locked?**  
A: After 5 failed login attempts, accounts are temporarily locked for 30 minutes.

---

### Booking & Payment

**Q: Can I book for someone else?**  
A: Yes! Just enter their details as the passenger during booking.

**Q: My payment failed. What should I do?**  
A: Wait for 24 hours for any held amount to be reversed, then try again. If issues persist, contact your bank.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**  
A: Yes, we use Stripe for secure payments. Your card details are never stored on our servers.

**Q: Can I change my flight date after booking?**  
A: Changes depend on fare rules. Check "Manage Booking" for available options and applicable fees.

---

### Cancellation & Refunds

**Q: How long does a refund take?**  
A: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. Credit to your account may take additional time depending on your bank.

**Q: Can I get a full refund?**  
A: Full refunds depend on fare type and how early you cancel. Check our cancellation policy for details.

**Q: I didn't receive my refund. Who do I contact?**  
A: Check your email for refund confirmation. If you haven't received it within 10 business days, contact support.

---

### Check-In & Boarding

**Q: What documents do I need at the airport?**  
A: Valid photo ID (Aadhaar, Passport, or Voter ID) and your boarding pass (digital or printed).

**Q: I missed web check-in. Can I still fly?**  
A: Yes, you can check in at the airport counter. Arrive at least 2 hours early.

**Q: Can I change seats after check-in?**  
A: Seat changes after check-in are subject to availability. Contact airline staff at the airport.
